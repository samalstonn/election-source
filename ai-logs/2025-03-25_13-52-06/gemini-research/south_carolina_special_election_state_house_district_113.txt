Here's a comprehensive overview of the South Carolina Special Election for State House District 113, scheduled for March 25, 2025.

**1. Positions Up For Election**

*   **Position Name:** State Representative for District 113
*   **Election Date:** March 25, 2025
*   **City and State:** District 113 represents portions of North Charleston and Summerville, South Carolina.
*   **Description:** A State Representative is a member of the South Carolina House of Representatives, the lower chamber of the South Carolina General Assembly. They are responsible for:
    *   Passing bills on public policy matters.
    *   Setting levels for state spending.
    *   Raising and lowering taxes.
    *   Voting to uphold or override gubernatorial vetoes.
*   **Category:** State

**2. Candidates Running for State Representative, District 113**

*   **Note:** As of March 25, 2025, Courtney Waters won the special Democratic primary and is running in the special general election. Since no Republican candidates filed, Waters will face write-in opposition in the March 25 election.
